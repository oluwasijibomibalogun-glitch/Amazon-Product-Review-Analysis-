# Amazon-Product-Review-Analysis-

## Description
This Power BI project analyzes Amazon product and customer review data to generate actionable insights. The dashboard shows:
•	Product pricing and discount strategies
•	Customer satisfaction trends
•	Review and rating patterns
•	Potential revenue opportunities

## Project Overview
The project focuses on understanding Amazon product and customer review data. The main goals:
•	Identify which product categories generate the most revenue
•	Examine the relationship between discounts and ratings
•	Highlight top-performing products based on reviews and ratings
•	Analyze pricing strategies and potential revenue

The dashboard allows interactive filtering by category, price bucket, ratings, and discount level.

•	Columns: 16
## Key Columns:
Product Details:
•	Product Name
•	Category

•	Discount %
•	Price Bucket


## Customer Engagement:
•	Rating
•	Rating Count
•	Review Titles
•	Review Content

## Calculated Metrics:
•	Average Discount
•	Products with ≥50% Discount
•	Potential Revenue
•	Combined Rating & Review Score

## Tools Used
•	Power BI Desktop
•	Data modelling: Calculated columns and measures
•	Visualizations: Cards, bar charts, line charts, scatter charts, and slicers
•	Interactive dashboard features for filtering and drill-through

## Data Cleaning / Preparation
•	Removed duplicates and invalid entries
•	Standardized product names and categories
•	Created calculated columns:
1.	Discount % = (Actual Price – Discounted Price) / Actual Price * 100
2.	Price Bucket = <200, 200–500, >500
3.	Potential Revenue = Actual Price * Rating Count
•	Grouped discount and rating values into buckets for visualization

•	Comparison charts for ratings vs discount
•	Revenue-focused insights using calculated measures

## Key Findings
•	Total potential revenue was 81 billion, with most revenue from top-performing categories.
•	Electronics had the highest number of reviews.
•	Ratings mostly fall between 4.0 and 5.0.
•	More than 50% of products had discounts of 50% or more.

## Conclusion
Using Power BI, this project demonstrates how raw e-commerce data can be transformed into actionable business insights.
The interactive dashboard allows quick decision-making for product pricing, marketing strategies, and customer engagement.
